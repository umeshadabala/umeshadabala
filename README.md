### 🚀 About Me

I am a teenage founder, developer, and builder based in Bangalore, India 🇮🇳. I am passionate about creating technology that addresses real-world challenges, particularly in urban mobility, accessibility, and developer tooling. 

- 🎓 **Founder & Developer** focused on building products designed for real-world impact.
- 🏆 **India Book of Records (IBR) Achiever** for **[BhashaBytes](https://github.com/BhashaBytes)**.
- 🏢 Building **[ParkoSpace](https://github.com/ParkoSpace)** to redefine peer-to-peer parking in India.
- 💻 Exploring startup growth, cloud-native architectures, and Agentic AI systems.
- 📝 Writing tech insights and articles at **[dev.to/umeshlab](https://dev.to/umeshlab/)**.
